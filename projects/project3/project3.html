ABOUT WATERBOMB

WaterBomb is an AR Event Development project with AR technology, mixing 2D and 3D objects.

The briefing was to create a poster for a festival and we chose the WaterBomb festival, which takes place in different cities, the main one being Seoul. Every year their visual identity changes and we felt the need to research these different identities to come up with one that made sense.
