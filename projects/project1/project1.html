ABOUT BUNSSY

Bunssy is an ux/ui project and innovative brand dedicated to the care of rabbits, promoting health, well-being and happiness for these small animals and their guardians.

The brand focuses on educating about bunny care, a sustainable and ethical approach, and promoting products recommended by health professionals.
